Halo-Halo

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sweetened jackfruit (langka), sliced
  • 1/2 cup sweetened banana (saba), sliced
  • 1/2 cup sweetened red mung beans
  • 1/2 cup sweetened garbanzo beans (chickpeas)
  • 1/2 cup macapuno (sweetened coconut strips)
  • 1/2 cup nata de coco (coconut gel)
  • 1/2 cup kaong (sugar palm fruit)
  • 1/2 cup purple yam (ube) halaya (jam)
  • 1/2 cup leche flan, sliced or cubed
  • 1/4 cup pinipig (toasted rice flakes) or crushed cornflakes
  • 1 scoop ube ice cream or vanilla ice cream (per serving)
  • Grated cheese (optional, for a creamy twist)

Instructions

  1. Step 1. If you donâ€™t have pre-shaved ice, crush ice cubes in a blender or use a manual ice shaver.
  2. Step 2. In tall glasses, layer 1-2 tablespoons of each topping, starting with the heavier items like sweetened beans, nata de coco, and kaong. Add a layer of shaved ice to fill the glass about three-quarters full. Pour 1/4 cup evaporated milk over the ice in each glass.
  3. Step 3. Top the ice with a dollop of ube halaya, a slice of leche flan, and a scoop of ube or vanilla ice cream. Sprinkle pinipig or crushed cornflakes over the top for crunch. Add grated cheese if desired for a savory hint.
  4. Step 4. Serve immediately with a long spoon. Instruct everyone to "halo-halo" (mix) all the ingredients before eating to enjoy the blend of textures and flavors!
